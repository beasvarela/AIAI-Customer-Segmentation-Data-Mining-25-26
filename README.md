

# Amazing International Airlines Inc.

## Project Overview

Amazing International Airlines Inc. (AIAI) is facing the challenge of designing personalized services and marketing strategies for its diverse customer base. In today’s highly competitive airline industry, leveraging data-driven approaches to understand customer segments is crucial for improving satisfaction, increasing retention, and maximizing revenue potential.

In this project, we acted as consultants for AIAI with the task of analyzing customer loyalty membership data and corresponding flight activity collected over a three-year period. Using these insights, we developed a data-driven segmentation strategy based on multiple perspectives:
- value-based segmentation, grouping customers according to their economic contribution;
- behavioral segmentation, analyzing purchasing habits and travel behaviors;
- demographic segmentation, categorizing customers by age, occupation, or other attributes to reveal different interaction patterns.

Additionally, geo-spatial insights were also included in the analysis.

## Clustering Methodologies:

- DBSCAN, HDBSCAN were used for outlier identification;
- **Value-Based**: hierarchical, cell-based, K-Means, Mean Shift, Self Organizing Maps (SOMs), SOM + K-Means, SOM + Hierarchical
- **Behavioral**: hierarchical, K-Means, Mean Shift, Self Organizing Maps (SOMs), SOM + K-Means, SOM + Hierarchical

An RFM analysis was also conducted on all customers.

Finally, the clusters found for each perspective were merged an visualized using techniques such as:
- PCA
- UMAP
- t-SNE

A final report was redacted, explaining the whole clustering process and including final considerations and strategic recommmendations for AIAI's business.
